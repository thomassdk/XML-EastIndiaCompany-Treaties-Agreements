SOONTH – SOUTHERN MURATHA COUNTRY.	761
Translation of a Note addressed by RANA BHIWANNE SINGJEE, of Soonth, to A. REMINGTON, Esquire, Officiating 1st Assistant Political Commissioner for Guzerat, dated 12th May 1840.
	Having taken into consideration the subject brought to my notice regarding the abolition of Suttee, I will make arrangements in my territory to prohibit the future observance of Suttee.
Seal of the Rana of Soonth. 
(True translation.)
(Signed) A. REMINGTON,
Officiating 1st Assistant Political Commissioner.
