WANKANEER – WARYE – WOW.	891
WANKANEER.
Translation of a Note addressed by THAKOOR SIRDAR SING, of Wankaneer, to A. REMINGTON, Esq., Officiating 1st Assistant Political Commissioner for Gurzerat, dated Vysack Shood 7th Sumvut 1896, or the 8th May 1840.
	Having taken into consideration the subject brought into my notice, regarding the abolition of Suttee, I will prohibit the practice in my Districts, and make suitable arrangements for its discontinuance.
(Signed)	SIRDAR SING.
(True translation)
(Signed)	A. REMINGTON,
Officiating 1st Assistant Political Commissioner.
