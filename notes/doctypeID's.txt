The doctypes are sorted into alphabetical order and are given a corresponding number below:

Note 		1
Paper 		2
Proclamation	3

The ID is then created using the acronymn from the title TAE (See note on document ID's) followed by DTY (An acronym for "Document TYpe") followed by doctypes corresponding number.

Eg. TAEDTY1 = A "Note" Document Type found in TREATIES, AGREEMENTS, AND ENGAGEMENTS, BETWEEN THE HONORABLE EAST INDIA COMPANY
